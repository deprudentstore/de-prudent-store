The WhatsApp Ordering Setup Checklist

Taking orders on WhatsApp looks simple until the messages pile up. Get the 5-step checklist to run it without losing your mind — or orders.

  • A menu link customers can share, not a screenshot
  • Order confirmation customers actually see
  • A delivery radius you've actually decided on
  • Payment confirmation before the food leaves
  • A system for repeat customers, not just new ones

No spam. One useful email, then occasional tips.
