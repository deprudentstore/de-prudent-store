🛒 Shopify Store – Complete Engagement Guide

How to build, manage, and profit from Shopify stores – from zero to live.

Your Role: Client pays for Shopify subscription. You get added as a Staff Member. You build the store. You collect your service fee separately.

1. Getting Client Access

  1. Client goes to Shopify Admin → Settings → Users & Permissions → Add Staff.
  2. They enter your email: deprudentstoredesigner@gmail.com and give you Full Permissions.
  3. You receive an invite email → Accept → You're in!

2. Step-by-Step Store Setup

Step 1 – Niche & Product Research

  • Understand client’s target audience, research 3 top competitors, and identify 10-20 starting products using Google Trends.

Step 2 – Account & Theme Setup

  • Start trial at shopify.com. Use a free theme (Dawn, Sense, Craft) or paid (Prestige $350, Impulse $380). Customize logo, colors, fonts.

Step 3 – Homepage & Navigation

  • Set up hero banner with CTA, featured collections, trust badges, main navigation (Home, Shop, About, Contact), and footer.

Step 4 – Product Pages

  • Add high-quality images (5+ per product), benefit-focused descriptions, variants (size/color), prices, and inventory.

Step 5 – Essential Pages

  • About Us, Contact, FAQ, Shipping Policy, Return & Refund Policy, Privacy Policy (auto-generated).

Step 6 – Payments

  • For Nigerian clients: Paystack or Flutterwave (via Shopify Apps). For international: PayPal, Stripe, or manual Bank Transfer.

Step 7 – Shipping & Apps

  • Set shipping zones & rates. Install essential apps: Judge.me (reviews), Tidio (chat), DSers (dropshipping), Mailchimp (email).

3. Headless Shopify (Your Premium Offer)

Headless Shopify means you build a custom React/HTML frontend that connects to Shopify as the backend for checkout and payments.

FeatureNormal ShopifyHeadless Shopify
FrontendShopify theme (limited)Custom HTML/React (unlimited)
DesignTemplate-basedFully custom pixel-perfect
Your Price$99 – $349$199 – $499

How to build Headless: Client creates Shopify store → Enables Storefront API → You copy the API token → Build custom frontend → Deploy on Netlify.

API Endpoint: https://{store-name}.myshopify.com/api/2024-01/graphql.json
